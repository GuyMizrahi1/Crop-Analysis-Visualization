N/ST Ratio: Phenology-Based Fertilization Indicator

Fertilize at ratio peaks (gradient changes + → -) | Peaks = high Nitrogen relative to depleted Starch

Why Fertilize at Peaks?
Ratio peaks indicate starch depletion relative to nitrogen - the tree is investing reserves in growth.
Fertilizing now replenishes nutrients when uptake is most efficient.
Winter (Dec-Jan)
N ↑ (remobilization) | ST ↓ → Ratio rises
Spring (Apr-May) - Peak
N ↓ | ST ↓↓ (flowering) → Highest peak
Summer (Aug)
N ↑ (uptake) | ST low → Ratio rises
