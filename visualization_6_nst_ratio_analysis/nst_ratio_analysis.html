N/ST Ratio: Phenology-Based Fertilization Indicator

Three fertilization windows aligned with citrus growth phases: December (dormancy break), April (spring flush), August (summer growth)

Key Insight: The N/ST ratio responds to phenological stages rather than calendar seasons. Local maxima in the ratio align with citrus growth phases, identifying optimal fertilization windows in December (post-dormancy), April (spring flush), and August (fruit development).
